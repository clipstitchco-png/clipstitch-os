Team rules

Professional standards are the foundation of a strong studio culture.

These rules help us work with clarity, respect, and consistency.

✦
Communication Use clear, timely, and thoughtful communication. Keep updates direct and useful.
✦
Deadlines Respect delivery timelines. When a risk appears, communicate early and propose a solution.
✦
Ownership Own your work from beginning to completion and follow through with accountability.
✦
Professionalism Maintain calm, thoughtful, and dependable behavior in all internal and client-facing work.
✦
Respect Treat teammates, clients, and collaborators with consideration and care.
✦
File organization Keep work structured, labeled clearly, and easy for others to access.
✦
Client communication policy Communicate with clarity, confidence, and professionalism. Keep expectations aligned.
✦
Approval policy Do not present work as final until it has been reviewed or approved by the right person.
