Project workflow

Projects move through a disciplined sequence designed to keep quality high and decisions clear.

The workflow is simple enough to follow and structured enough to scale.

Client

The engagement begins with the client need, context, and expectations.

Discovery

We gather information, align on goals, and define the scope of the work.

Strategy

We shape the narrative, positioning, and strategic direction behind the work.

Creative Brief

The brief translates strategy into a clear plan for execution.

Production

The team builds the core assets and deliverables required for the project.

Editing

We refine, tighten, and improve the work before final review.

Review

Stakeholders review the work, confirm direction, and approve the result.

Delivery

The final work is shared in a polished, organized, and client-ready format.
